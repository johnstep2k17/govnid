What is Cybersecurity.
A. The practice of locking only your house doors and windows.
B. The practice of protecting computers, phones, and networks from hackers, viruses, and scams.
C. The practice of buying new gadgets for protection.
D. The practice of using the internet only during the day.
ANSWER: B 

What are the components of the CIA Triad in Cybersecurity.
A. Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability
B. Communication, Integration, Authentication
C. Control, Investigation, Assurance
D. Confidentiality, Innovation, Access
ANSWER: A
